# Turndown Product Documentation

Turndown is a property operations platform for short-term rentals. It helps property managers organize properties, rooms, standards, jobs, supplies, and issues while giving cleaners and maintenance workers a much simpler day-to-day experience.

This documentation explains the product as a whole in plain English. It is written to describe **what the app is, what it contains, how people use it, and what information exists inside it**.

## What This Product Covers

Turndown currently models the following parts of a rental operations business:

- company setup and company-to-company relationships
- user accounts, team roles, and invitations
- property and room management
- reusable cleaning standards and room checklists
- scheduled and in-progress work sessions
- step-by-step checklist completion
- inventory tracking and restocking
- damage reporting and maintenance work orders
- photos and image-based documentation
- subscription and plan management

## Product Shape at a Glance

Turndown is best understood as **two connected experiences**:

1. **Property manager experience**
   - richer, more detailed, more administrative
   - centered on setup, oversight, quality control, and coordination

2. **Cleaner / maintenance experience**
   - faster, simpler, task-first
   - centered on what to do now, where to go, and what proof to capture
